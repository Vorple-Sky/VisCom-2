Hannah Gabany is a multimedia artist based out of San Francisco, California. Her work mainly consits of digital media and web design projects, occasionally indulging in photography and painting. She is currently studying design as her major and computer science as her minior at the University of San Francisco. Hannah is very intrested in video game character design and hopes to pursue this passion after completeing school. She was first drawn to the complexe character designs she saw within her games. Growing up with games connecting her to family and friends she aspires to create memorible characters for others to connect over in the future. Hannah grew up in Petaluma, California a small town north of San Francisco. The town is home to many large winerys and farming establishments helping her to build a strong love of community and quality work. Inspired by the sencery, but passionate about art and design she chose to attend USF to develope her skills as an artist and designer. On this site are images, digital copies, and scans of her work displaying her progress over the years. She continues to create art and educate herself in new ways to become the designer she hopes to be.
